FAQ
What is this project?
The Silk Road Mining (SRM) project was founded in 2018 by a team of specialists from different countries, mainly from China, Kazakhstan and the UAE, with the support of BITT Inc. We offer our clients the services of industrial mining on the territory of the special industrial zone in the North Kazakhstan region.
When will the project be launched?
The planned launch of the ICO will take place in the summer of 2018, and on January 1, 2019, investors will begin to receive their first dividends.
What do SRM depositors get?
SRM depositors will receive SILK tokens representing their share in the SRM project. These tokens give their owners the opportunity to receive 100% of the net profit from mining. 70% of them will be distributed as dividends among the token owners, 10% - to the team, the remaining 20% will be reinvested to match the growing complexity of mining and to upgrade the infrastructure.
What are SILK tokens?
The SILK token is a token based on the ERC20 standard of the Ethereum blockchain. It empowers the owner to receive a portion of the net profit from the activities of SILK ROAD MINING.
How to purchase SILK tokens?
SRM tokens can be purchased for BTC, ETH, LTC, BCH, Fiat (cash) funds*. The transfer can be made from any wallet to BTC, ETH, LTC, BCH. (ETH wallet must support ERC20 standard). *Fiat investors ' funds will be returned to the program in the form of tokens SILK under the assumption that 1SILK = 1USD.
When can I purchase SILK tokens?
Pre-sale dates: July 20, 2018-August 20, 2018.
Main sale dates: September 1, 2018-November 1, 2018.
Who can buy SILK tokens?
The offer will be open to everyone. In addition to residents of the United States.
What is the minimum investment amount?
The minimum investment amount is the equivalent of$ 100 in BTC, ETH, LTC, BCH.
What wallet to use for storing SILK tokens?
The investor will need an ERC20 compatible wallet to hold SILK tokens.
What happens to all unsold tokens?
Unsold tokens will be destroyed upon completion of the ICO.

How are dividends distributed?
SILK smart contract will automatically distribute dividends to all SILK token holders according to their percentage ownership of the number of tokens sold during the entire ICO period.
